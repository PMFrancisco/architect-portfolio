## Concept

Nexus Tower represents the new generation of corporate buildings in Spain, where sustainability is not an add-on but the guiding principle of design.

## Consulting Scope

- Executive architectural design
- BIM coordination with all specialties
- LEED certification management
- Specialized facade supervision

## Impact

The building consumes 45% less energy than baseline code and has been recognized with the 2023 sustainable architecture award.
