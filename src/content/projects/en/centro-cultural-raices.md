## Concept

The Raíces Cultural Center seeks to be a bridge between Seville's past and present, celebrating Andalusia's rich artisanal tradition while offering spaces for contemporary creation.

## Consulting Services

- Heritage impact studies
- Participatory design with the community
- Historical Heritage coordination
- Traditional materials specification
